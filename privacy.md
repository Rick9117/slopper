# Privacy Policy for Slopper

_Last updated: 19 July 2026_

## Summary

Slopper does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no
accounts, no servers, and no analytics.

## What Slopper stores

Slopper saves the following on your own device only, using Chrome's local
storage (`chrome.storage.local`):

- Your settings: selected mode (Mom or Dad), whether the extension is enabled,
  the NSFW language toggle, and your daily time limit.
- Your daily totals: the number of seconds spent on each supported platform
  (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, X), which reset each day.

This information never leaves your device. It is not sent to the developer or
to any third party.

## What Slopper does not store

Slopper does not record the URLs you visit, page titles, browsing history,
video titles, personal information, or the contents of any page.

## Permissions

- **storage** — saves your settings and daily totals locally on your device.
- **tabs** — used only to close a tab in Dad mode once your time limit has
  passed. No tab data is read or stored.
- **Host permissions** (youtube.com, instagram.com, tiktok.com, facebook.com,
  twitter.com, x.com) — allow the extension to check whether you are currently
  watching short-form video on these sites, in order to measure viewing time
  and display the limit overlay.

## Removing your data

Uninstalling Slopper removes all stored data. You can also clear it at any time
through Chrome's extension settings.
